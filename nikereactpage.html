Nike React

Nike react is a foam made also by Nike inc. They made it because Nike needed a foam to compete with the Adidas Ultra boost stealing
the market by storm this was released first - not as a running shoe - but a basketball shoe in 2017. A sport that requires players to shift
direction and speed in seamless motion and to lift off at the blink of an eye. The foam met those needs by providing a sensation that is
as soft and springy as it is squishy and stable. Nike lab researchers achieved this innovative combination after 400 combinations of
chemistry and processing and used the scientific method to find the best possible combination for this foam.

After the basketball shoes it was certain that it was brought into running and was uncaged from it's sealant in the midsole and was
exposed in its glory to show its full potential on the track. Research from Nike Lab suggests that after testing against other undisclosed
running foams that Nike React had the greatest energy return out from all of them. The Nike React alone provides already 13% greater
energy return than their latest innovation before this the Lunarlon.
